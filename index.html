⚠️ Privacy & Data Disclosure

When you click Analyze, the plain text of all your journal entries is sent to the selected AI provider's API for processing. This means:

  • Your entries leave your device and are transmitted to a third-party server.
  • Each provider has its own data retention and privacy policy — please review them.
  • Your API key is stored only in your browser's localStorage and is never shared with anyone other than the provider you choose.
  • No data is collected by this app. The app has no backend — it is 100% client-side.
